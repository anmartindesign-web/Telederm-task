→ Production Notes
Color Grade
Two looks: cool/desaturated for pain, warm/bright after "Telederm comes in." This visual shift does half the storytelling work automatically.
Text Overlays
Captions needed during all the video - some text can be emphasised additionally with animation/effects
Pacing
Pain section: 3–4 s per shot. How it works: 1.5–2 s. CTA: pace quickens, then holds on the doctor's final shot. Rhythm = emotion.
B-Roll Casting
Diverse skin tones among b-roll subjects — especially important for a dermatology brand. Signals inclusivity and builds broader trust.
Audio Mixing
Doctor's voice: –12 dBFS. Music under speech: –22 to –26 dBFS. Music only: –16 dBFS. Voice always wins the competition with music.
End Card
Minimum 5–8 seconds. Logo, URL/CTA, optional short tagline. One action only: "Start Your Visit Today."
